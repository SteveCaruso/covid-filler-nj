What is CoVID Injection?

A tool that lets volunteers enter information into online CoVID vaccination portals faster than can be done by hand, and without the long process of pre-loading a web browser's autofill feature.

The above video shows how quickly it takes to get to the part of CVS' form where an appointment is reserved for 20 minutes. It takes only a few more clicks to actually confirm it (roughly another 4-5 seconds).

How Do I Uninstall It?

When you want to get rid of it, simply delete the bookmark. That's it.

How Can I Use It?

After configuration, basic operation requires three easy steps:

  1. Copy the line of the spreadsheet that has the user's information you wish to sign up with into your clipboard.
  2. Go to the vaccine portal.
  3. Click on the bookmarklet you put in your bookmarks bar and follow the instructions.

Advanced operation can be found on the bookmarklet generator page here.

Which Vaccine Portals Are Supported?

Presently only these portals are supported:

  • Rite Aid
  • CVS
  • Atlantic Health (90% support)
  • Most Curogram-based signups.
